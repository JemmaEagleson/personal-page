I'm an IxD student studying in Belfast with a love of simplicity, good design and music. I believe digital technology should be a pleasure to use as well as a beautiful piece of artwork to look at.

STUFF ABOUT ME

I'm currently in my 1st year of ixD at Belfast Art College. I live in Belfast and love checking out the local art scene and visiting as many exhibitions as I can!

I love to inject creative cool into all my projects and allow my ideas to come alive both on screen and on paper. I take inspiration from objects and scenery around me as well as from some of my favourite artists and designers.
